❓ Frequently Asked Questions

I requested a game. When will it be added?

We review game requests weekly. If the game is HTML5 compatible, school-appropriate, and not already in our library, we typically add it within 3-5 days. We'll email you when it's live!

Why is a game showing a blank screen?

Your school's firewall may have blocked the specific server we're using. Please report the exact game to us at support@unblockedgames-67.github.io so we can update the proxy link within 24 hours.

Can I lose my save data?

Yes. Game saves are stored in your browser's local storage. If you clear your browsing data, use incognito mode, or switch devices, your progress will be lost. We recommend not clearing browser history if you want to keep saves.

Are these games safe?

Absolutely. We rigorously screen all embedded content for malware, viruses, and intrusive ads. Every game is tested before it goes live. We also display only trusted ad networks like Google AdSense.

Do I need to create an account?

Never! Unblocked Games 67 has zero registration requirements. Just click and play. No email sign-ups, no passwords to remember, no personal data collected.

Why are there ads?

Ads cover our server costs and keep the site 100% free for everyone. We use non-intrusive display ads that won't interrupt your gameplay. No pop-ups, no autoplay videos.
