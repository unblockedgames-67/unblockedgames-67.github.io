🏭 Unblocked Strategy Games: Progress While You Study

Strategy games on Unblocked Games 67 offer something unique: they reward patience and planning. Unlike reflex-based action games, strategy titles let you think, optimize, and watch your empire grow — even while you're supposed to be taking notes. Our collection includes idle clickers that progress in the background, life simulations that tell stories, and management games that test your economic skills. All 15+ titles are unblocked and optimized for Chromebooks.

⏱️ The Idle Game Advantage

Idle and clicker games are perfect for school environments:

  • Background progression: Cookie Clicker keeps baking even when you switch tabs to study
  • Minimal interaction: Check in occasionally, optimize your setup, then return to work
  • No network flags: Single-player focus means no multiplayer ports to trigger filters
  • Psychological satisfaction: Watching numbers grow is scientifically rewarding

📊 Complete Strategy Collection

  • Cookie Clicker: The game that started a genre. Click to bake cookies, purchase upgrades, and eventually produce cookies through automated systems. Grandmas, farms, factories, time machines — it's deceptively deep. Current world record: over 1 billion cookies per second.
  • BitLife: Live a virtual life. Make choices about education, career, relationships, and crime. Every decision has consequences. Try to become a doctor, a criminal, or just live a quiet life. No two playthroughs are the same.
  • Monkey Mart: The cutest management sim. Grow crops, stock shelves, hire employees, and expand your supermarket. Relaxing yet addictive — perfect for unwinding between classes.
  • Learn to Fly 3: Progression game with research and testing. Build a flying machine, launch, fail, research upgrades, try again. Multiple endings, hidden content, and actual educational value about physics.
  • Supermarket Simulator: More realistic store management. Stock shelves, manage inventory, set prices, handle customers. For players who want deeper economic simulation.
  • Age of War: Tower defense meets troop management. Evolve through eras from cavemen to the future while defending your base and attacking the enemy.
  • Craftmine: Browser-based Minecraft-like experience. Mine resources, craft tools, build structures. Surprisingly deep for a browser game.
  • Space Plan: Factory building and optimization. Mine resources, build conveyor belts, automate production. Factorio-like gameplay in your browser.

💾 How Progress Saves on School Chromebooks

School devices often clear cache between sessions. Our strategy games use multiple methods to keep your progress:

  • Local storage: Data stays in your browser's dedicated storage, surviving restarts
  • Export codes: Cookie Clicker and similar games let you export save codes — copy to a document, import later
  • Browser cache: Some games use traditional caching that persists through reboots
  • No cloud required: Your progress stays private on your device — no accounts needed
