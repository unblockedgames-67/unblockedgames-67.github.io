1. Our Privacy Commitment

Your privacy is critically important to us at Unblocked Games 67. Because our primary audience includes students playing on school networks, we have designed our platform to collect the absolute minimum data necessary to function. This Privacy Policy explains exactly what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

✅ The Short Version: We don't sell your data. We don't create accounts. We don't track you across the web. Game saves stay on your device.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Unblocked Games 67 is a no-registration website. We do NOT collect:

  • ❌ Personal identifiers: Names, addresses, phone numbers, or birth dates
  • ❌ Account credentials: We have no user accounts or passwords
  • ❌ Payment information: Our service is completely free
  • ❌ Precise location data: No GPS or geolocation tracking
  • ❌ Biometric or sensitive data: None whatsoever

3. Information We Automatically Collect

Like virtually all websites, our servers and analytics tools automatically collect basic technical information to maintain and improve the Service:

3.1 Device and Browser Information

  • Browser type and version (e.g., Chrome 122, Safari 17)
  • Operating system (e.g., ChromeOS, Windows 11, macOS)
  • Screen resolution and device type (desktop, tablet, mobile)
  • Language preferences

3.2 Network Information

  • Anonymized IP addresses — We mask the last octet (e.g., 192.168.1.xxx) so individual users cannot be identified
  • Referral source — How you found our site (search engine, direct link, etc.)
  • Page load times and performance metrics

3.3 Usage Information

  • Most popular games — Which games are played most frequently
  • Session duration — How long users typically stay on the site
  • Navigation patterns — How users move through our pages

This information is aggregated and anonymized. We cannot identify individual users from this data.

4. Information You Voluntarily Provide

If you use our Contact Form to report a bug, request a game, or ask a question, we collect:

  • The email address you provide (so we can reply)
  • The name or alias you provide
  • The content of your message

These emails are stored securely and deleted periodically (typically within 60 days). We never use these emails for marketing purposes.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Unblocked Games 67 uses two types of browser storage:

5.1 HTML5 Local Storage (Game Saves)

Many HTML5 games (including Retro Bowl, Cookie Clicker, and BitLife) use your browser's local storage to save your game progress. This data:

  • Stays on your device — we do not access or store it on our servers
  • Persists until you clear your browser data
  • Is specific to the browser and device you're using

5.2 Cookies (Analytics and Advertising)

We use cookies from trusted third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand site traffic and improve user experience. Data is anonymized.
  • Google AdSense: Displays relevant ads to keep our service free. Uses cookies to prevent showing the same ad repeatedly and to measure ad performance.

Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies

Blocking cookies may affect site functionality and game saves.

6. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. This is called personalized advertising.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance Choices

AdSense also uses the DoubleClick cookie to enable frequency capping and aggregated ad reporting. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices here.

7. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Unblocked Games 67 is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

  • Our website is designed to function without collecting personal information
  • If we discover that a child under 13 has submitted an email address via our contact form, we will delete it immediately
  • We encourage parents to monitor their children's online activities and to contact us if they have concerns

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/HTTPS encryption: All traffic between your browser and our site is encrypted
  • Regular security audits: We monitor for vulnerabilities
  • Minimal data collection: The best way to protect data is not to collect it

9. International Users

Unblocked Games 67 is hosted in the United States. If you access our site from outside the US, your data may be transferred to and processed in the US. By using our Service, you consent to this transfer.

10. Your Rights (GDPR and CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

For European Users (GDPR):

  • Right to access — request what data we hold (though we hold very little)
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of any data we might have
  • Right to object — opt out of cookies via browser settings

For California Users (CCPA):

  • Right to know — what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete — request deletion of personal information
  • Right to opt out — we do not sell personal information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@unblockedgames-67.github.io.
