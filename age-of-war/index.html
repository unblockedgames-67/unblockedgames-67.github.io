Advanced Strategies For Age Of War

Want to dominate every age? These Age Of War tips will transform your gameplay:

Balance Offense And Defense

Many new players focus entirely on offense, neglecting base defense. While your units attack the enemy base, their units attack yours. Maintain a balanced force—send enough attackers to pressure the enemy while keeping defenders to protect your base. The ideal ratio is roughly 60% offense, 40% defense, adjusted based on enemy aggression.

Evolution Timing

Don't rush evolution immediately. Sometimes staying in a lower age longer lets you build a stronger economy. Each age has optimal unit production rates. Consider delaying evolution until you have maximum units for your current age, then evolve to immediately deploy upgraded versions.

Unit Synergies

Different units complement each other. In Medieval Age, knights absorb damage while crossbowmen attack from behind. In Modern Age, artillery destroys enemy defenses while riflemen engage troops. Experiment with combinations to find what works against specific enemy compositions.

Defensive Positioning

Your base has specific defensive positions. Place ranged defenders where they can attack approaching enemies while staying protected. Melee defenders work best near the front lines. Upgrade defensive structures before they're destroyed—once gone, they're expensive to rebuild.
