Advanced Strategies For Drift Hunters

Want to become the ultimate drifter? These Drift Hunters tips will improve your game:

Master The Initiation

A good drift starts with proper initiation. Approach the corner at speed, then use the handbrake (Spacebar) to break rear traction while turning. Feather the throttle to maintain the slide—too much gas and you'll spin out, too little and you'll straighten [citation:3].

Look Through The Corner

Don't stare at the wall in front of you. Look ahead to where you want to go. Your car naturally follows your eyes. Looking through the corner helps you anticipate the exit and maintain a smooth line.

Experiment with tuning settings to match your driving style. Softer suspension for smoother tracks, stiffer for aggressive transitions. More rear camber helps initiate drifts easier. Save different setups for different tracks [citation:9].
