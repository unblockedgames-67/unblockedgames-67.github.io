1. Our Commitment to Copyright Compliance

Unblocked Games 67 respects the intellectual property rights of game developers, publishers, and creators. We comply fully with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and international copyright laws. As a gaming portal that embeds HTML5 games from various sources, we take copyright claims seriously and respond promptly to valid takedown notices.

If you are a copyright owner (or an authorized agent) and believe that any content on our site infringes your copyrights, we will act quickly to remove or disable access to the material upon receipt of a valid DMCA notice.

⚠️ Important: Before filing a DMCA notice, please ensure the game is actually hosted on our servers. Many games are embedded from third-party platforms. If the game is hosted elsewhere, please contact that platform directly.

2. How to File a DMCA Takedown Notice

To request removal of infringing material, please send a formal written notice to our Designated DMCA Agent containing the following information (required by 17 U.S.C. § 512(c)(3)):

Required Information:

  1. Identification of the copyrighted work: Describe or provide the title of the copyrighted work you believe has been infringed.
  2. Identification of infringing material: Provide the exact URL(s) on Unblocked Games 67 where the allegedly infringing game or content is located.
  3. Your contact information: Include your full legal name, physical address, telephone number, and email address.
  4. Good faith statement: Include the exact phrase: "I have a good faith belief that the use of the copyrighted material described above is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law."
  5. Accuracy and perjury statement: Include the exact phrase: "I swear, under penalty of perjury, that the information in this notification is accurate and that I am the copyright owner or am authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed."
  6. Your signature: Physical or electronic signature (typing your full name constitutes electronic signature).

4. What Happens After Submission?

  1. Acknowledgment: We will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours.
  2. Review: Our team reviews your notice for completeness and validity.
  3. Action: If valid, we will remove or disable access to the identified content within 48 business hours.
  4. Notification: We may notify the party who posted the content (if identifiable) that a takedown notice has been received.

5. Counter-Notification Procedure

If you believe your content was removed by mistake or misidentification, you may submit a Counter-Notice. To be effective, a Counter-Notice must include:

  1. Identification of the material that was removed and its location before removal.
  2. A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed as a result of mistake or misidentification.
  3. Your name, address, and telephone number.
  4. A statement consenting to the jurisdiction of the Federal District Court for the judicial district in which your address is located (or, if outside the US, consenting to jurisdiction in the United States District Court for the District of Delaware).
  5. Your physical or electronic signature.

Send Counter-Notices to the same contact methods above. Upon receipt of a valid Counter-Notice, we may restore the removed content in accordance with DMCA procedures.

6. Repeat Infringer Policy

In accordance with the DMCA, Unblocked Games 67 maintains a policy to terminate, in appropriate circumstances, the accounts of users or third-party sources who are deemed repeat infringers. We also reserve the right to block access to content from sources that consistently generate infringement claims.

7. Legal Warning

⚠️ IMPORTANT LEGAL NOTICE

Under Section 512(f) of the DMCA, any person who knowingly materially misrepresents that material is infringing or that material was removed by mistake may be subject to liability for damages (including costs and attorneys' fees). If you are unsure whether material infringes your copyright, please consult an attorney before submitting a notice.

8. Non-US Copyright Claims

While we primarily follow DMCA procedures for US-based claims, we respect international copyright laws. If you are located outside the United States, please include information about your jurisdiction and applicable copyright laws in your notice. We will review international claims and respond appropriately.
