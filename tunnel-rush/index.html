Advanced Strategies For Tunnel Rush

Want to achieve epic distances? These Tunnel Rush tips will extend your runs:

Look Ahead, Not At Your Orb

Focus your eyes on the distant tunnel, not directly on your orb . Your peripheral vision tracks your position while your central vision spots upcoming obstacles. This gives you maximum reaction time to plan your path .

Stay Centered When Possible

The center of the tunnel gives you equal movement options left or right. Edge positions limit your ability to dodge obstacles appearing on your side. Return to center after navigating each challenge .

Make Small, Precise Adjustments

Large, jerky movements often overcorrect and lead to crashes. Use gentle taps rather than held movements for finer control. Smooth positioning beats frantic dodging .

Anticipate Tunnel Twists

The tunnel twists in predictable patterns. Learn to anticipate which direction the tunnel will turn next. This lets you pre-position yourself for upcoming obstacles .
