Advanced Strategies For Bob The Robber 4

Want to become the ultimate thief? These Bob The Robber 4 tips will boost your success rate:

Patience Is A Virtue

Rushing causes mistakes. Watch guard patrols for at least one full cycle before moving. Identify blind spots and safe waiting areas. Sometimes waiting 30 seconds saves restarting the entire mission.

Learn Guard Patterns

Guards follow predictable routes but may pause or change direction at specific points. Memorize these patterns to time your movements perfectly. In later levels, guards have randomized elements—watch longer before committing.

Prioritize Upgrades

Don't waste money on flashy tools. Buy lockpicks first (essential), then hacking device, then silent shoes. Night vision is only useful in specific dark levels—purchase only when needed.

Use Shadows Strategically

Dark areas provide concealment but limit visibility. Position yourself in shadows near guard patrol paths, then move when guards face away. Some shadows are safer than others—test boundaries.
