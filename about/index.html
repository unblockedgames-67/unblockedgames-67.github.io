Our Story

Welcome to Unblocked Games 67. We launched this platform in early 2024 with a simple observation: school and workplace networks had become digital fortresses, blocking almost all forms of entertainment. We realized that during a stressful study hall, a lunch break, or a slow day at the office, a quick 10-minute gaming session is exactly what the brain needs to reset and regain focus.

Created by a small team of web developers and lifelong gamers, our platform was engineered from day one to bypass aggressive firewalls safely and reliably. We specialize in curating high-quality HTML5 games that run flawlessly on standard-issue hardware, especially the Chromebooks that dominate educational environments.

🎯 Our Mission

"To provide the most reliable, fastest-loading, and proxy-friendly gaming environment on the web, ensuring nobody is locked out of their favorite games."

The Power of Play

We firmly believe that gaming is not a waste of time. Research shows that short, focused bursts of gaming:

  • Improve hand-eye coordination — Fast-paced games train reflexes
  • Enhance problem-solving skills — Puzzle and strategy games build critical thinking
  • Reduce stress — A mental break increases productivity
  • Build community — Sharing high scores with friends creates connection

A quick run in Subway Surfers or a challenging level in Bloxorz can be the perfect palate cleanser for a tired mind.
